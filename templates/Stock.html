Despite impact of COVID-19 the total revenue for 2020 was $6.0 billion, an increase of 7.1% from 2019.
The increase in total revenue was driven by a 1.8% increase in comparable restaurant sales and new restaurant openings.
In 2020 we opened 161 new restaurants during the year.
Digital sales grew 174.1% year over year to $2.8 billion and represented 46.2% of sales. About half of the digital sales were via delivery, benefiting from our expanded partnerships.
